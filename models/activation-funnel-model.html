The three highlighted stages are the only ones my three proposed fixes touch — every other rate is held identical across both scenarios, so the gap at the bottom is attributable to the fixes and nothing else.

These inputs are illustrative defaults, not measured rates. I don't have Subspace's funnel data — the point of this tool is the shape of the loss and which stages carry it, not any specific figure. Drag every slider to whatever your real numbers are; the model recomputes live.
